Travel App

Pack Your Paws

This is Pack your Paws, me concept for an app that allows the user to easly travel with pets. I wanted to design an app for pet lovers that gives the user easy access to pet friendly travel locations, hotels activities etc. I have found that there is not many apps in this area. Pack Your Paws was designed to create a place for you and your pet while traveling. The tools that I have used for this project were, Adobe Illustrator amd Invision.

Gathering ideas on paper is where the desig process starts. I started with a mindmap of traveling, which gave me many ideas but after narrowing down to a few favourites I decided to go with traveling with pets. When the initial idea was there I started developing the concept of an app for traveling with pets. I decided on key categories for the app which would be hotels, activities and food. I started another mindmap to find a name for my app. I always struggle with this part of the design and it is one of the most important ones. The name has to be catchy and easy to understand. I decided to go with Pack Your Paws.

Before drawing ideas didgitaly, I always start on paper with some basic wireframing for the layout of the screens and for my icons. This helps me visualise on where to start when I move on to my computer.

Icons

After sketching on paper I moved on to digital drawing to create some of the icons that I will be using in Pack Your Paws. I wanted the illustration theme to be consistent throughout the app.

More Icons!

I started to put all the screens together to represent what Pack Your Paws would look like on the screen. The large amount of icons and illustrations makes the app easy to use without too much overwhelming information, limiting to most important like the information on hotels, activities and places to eat that are pet friendly. The app allows the user to find a hotel that is pet friendly and book it strreight away. SHowing the location and reviews of the places searched. The app limits to pet firendly only.
